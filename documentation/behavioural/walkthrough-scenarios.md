# CyberPulse Walkthrough Scenarios

## Scenario 1 - Visitor discovers CyberPulse
Sarah is a cybersecurity student who finds a CyberPulse article shared on LinkedIn. She clicks the link and lands on the homepage. She browses three free articles on OWASP Top 10 without logging in. On the fourth article she gets an upgrade prompt. She clicks Register and fills in her details.

## Scenario 2 - Subscriber logs in with MFA
James is a registered free subscriber. He navigates to CyberPulse and enters his email and password. The system validates his credentials and immediately prompts him for a one-time code. James opens his authenticator app, enters the 6-digit code, and is granted access.

## Scenario 3 - Subscriber manages premium subscription
James clicks Upgrade to Premium. He is taken to the subscription page where he selects the monthly plan at 9.99 euro, enters his payment details, and confirms. The system processes the payment and upgrades his account.

## Scenario 4 - Premium subscriber chats with a specialist
James has a question about penetration testing. He navigates to the Chat section and selects a verified specialist. He sends his question and receives a reply within the hour.

## Scenario 5 - Specialist publishes an article
Dr. Priya Nair logs in to CyberPulse with her verified specialist account. She drafts a new post on AWS IAM misconfigurations and submits for publication. The admin approves it the next morning.

## Scenario 6 - Specialist applies for verified badge
Kevin is a senior SOC analyst who wants to publish on CyberPulse. He registers and navigates to Apply for Verified Badge. He uploads his certifications and bio. The admin reviews and approves his application.

## Scenario 7 - Admin manages users and moderates content
Maria logs in to the admin dashboard. She reviews a flagged article and rejects it. She then suspends a user account for spamming and logs the action in the audit trail.
